# Unit Brand Identity

## Logo

The Unit logo features four stylized human figures arranged in a square formation, connected at their hands to form a circular bond. The figures are rendered in a gradient palette ranging from dark navy to light steel blue, symbolizing community, connection, and collaboration among tenants.

Below the icon, the wordmark **Unit** is displayed in a bold, modern sans-serif typeface with a gradient from dark navy to light steel blue. The tagline **"Where Tenants Connect"** appears beneath in an italic style using the slate blue tone.

### Logo Variants
- **Full logo**: Icon + wordmark + tagline
- **Icon only**: Four-figure mark (for app icons, favicons)
- **Wordmark only**: "Unit" text (for compact layouts)

### Logo Usage Guidelines
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "U" in the wordmark on all sides
- Do not stretch, rotate, or alter the logo proportions
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Preferred background: white or Soft Light Gray (#E0E1DE)

## Tagline

**"Where Tenants Connect"**

### Color Usage

#### Primary Actions & Headers
Use **Dark Navy (#101B29)** for primary buttons, navigation bars, headings, and key interactive elements. This is the anchor color of the brand.

#### Secondary Surfaces
Use **Deep Blue (#1D263A)** for secondary backgrounds, sidebar/navigation panels, and hover states on dark surfaces.

#### Supporting Elements
Use **Slate Blue (#465A75)** for body text on light backgrounds, icons, secondary buttons, and supporting UI elements.

#### Muted & Subtle
Use **Light Steel Blue (#7C8DA7)** for placeholder text, borders, disabled states, and muted information.

#### Backgrounds & Surfaces
Use **Soft Light Gray (#E0E1DE)** for page backgrounds, card surfaces, and dividers. White (#FFFFFF) remains the primary background.

### Gradients
The brand supports a left-to-right or top-to-bottom gradient:
- **Brand gradient**: Dark Navy (#101B29) -> Deep Blue (#1D263A) -> Slate Blue (#465A75) -> Light Steel Blue (#7C8DA7)
- **Subtle gradient**: Slate Blue (#465A75) -> Light Steel Blue (#7C8DA7) (for lighter UI accents)

### Accessibility Notes
- Dark Navy on white background: contrast ratio 17.3:1 (AAA)
- Slate Blue on white background: contrast ratio 7.0:1 (AA for normal text, AAA for large text)
- Light Steel Blue on white background: contrast ratio 3.4:1 (use only for large text or decorative elements)
- White text on Dark Navy: contrast ratio 17.3:1 (AAA)
- White text on Deep Blue: contrast ratio 15.1:1 (AAA)

## Typography

The logo uses a bold, modern sans-serif typeface. The application uses the system's default sans-serif stack through Tailwind CSS for optimal performance and native feel across platforms.

## Brand Voice

- **Community-focused**: Emphasize connection, collaboration, and shared spaces
- **Professional yet approachable**: Business-oriented but warm and welcoming
- **Clear and direct**: Simple language, no jargon

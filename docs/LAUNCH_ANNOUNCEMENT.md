# UNIT: The Tenant App is Here! 🏢📱

Dear [Building Name] Community,

We're incredibly excited to announce the launch of **UNIT** — the brand new mobile app built exclusively for our tenants!

Whether you want to discover other businesses in the building, post announcements, or grab exclusive local deals, UNIT is your all-in-one platform for property networking.

### What You Can Do in UNIT:

🔍 **Tenant Directory**
Instantly discover who else is in the building. Find services, explore profiles, and connect directly with other local business owners.

🛍️ **Exclusive Promotions**
Access a curated feed of special offers—both from other tenants in the building and local businesses right around the corner.

📅 **Community Board**
Host a networking event, announce a new service, or broadcast a building-wide update. It’s your internal bulletin board.

🔔 **Real-Time Push Notifications**
Never miss out. Get instant alerts when a new offer is posted or when building management sends a critical update.

### How to Get Started:

1. **Download the App:** Available now on the [App Store] and [Google Play Store].
2. **Login:** Use the temporary password sent to you in your welcome email.
3. **Set Up Your Profile:** Add your logo, update your business description, and list your services.
4. **Post Your First Offer:** Give the building a reason to come say hi!

We can't wait to see how you use UNIT to grow your business and connect with your neighbors.

— **The Management Team**
